Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2, 2025

1. Introduction

At Freaky Font Generator ("we," "our," "us"), your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights when using https://freakyfont.netlify.app.

2. Information We Collect

a. Information You Provide

  • Text Input: Any text you enter into our converter is processed locally in your browser and is never sent to our servers or stored anywhere. Your input remains private and under your control.

b. Information Collected Automatically

  • Cookies: Used by Google AdSense to deliver advertisements.
  • Usage Data: We may collect limited technical data such as IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on the site to improve functionality.
  • Device Information: Basic device details may be collected for optimization purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our Service.
  • Display relevant ads via Google AdSense.
  • Understand how users interact with our site.
  • Detect and prevent technical issues.
  • Comply with legal requirements.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Google AdSense to display ads. Google may use cookies or similar technologies to serve personalized ads based on your browsing activity.

You may opt out of personalized ads by adjusting your Google Ads Settings.

For details, see Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • Local Processing: All text conversion occurs in your browser. We do not transmit or store your input text.
  • No User Accounts: We do not require registration or maintain user profiles.
  • Security Measures: We use reasonable safeguards to protect any collected technical data from unauthorized access or alteration.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request details about any data we collect.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (though we collect minimal personal data).
  • Opt-Out: Disable personalized ads via Google's settings.
  • Do Not Track: Activate your browser's Do Not Track feature.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed toward children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe a child has submitted personal data, please contact us immediately.

8. International Users

If you access the Service from outside the United States, please note that your information may be processed in the United States, where data protection laws may differ from those in your region.

9. Regional Privacy Rights

California (CCPA)

California residents have the right to know, delete, and opt out of the sale of personal information without facing discrimination.

European Union (GDPR)

Users in the EU have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, object to processing, and request data portability.
